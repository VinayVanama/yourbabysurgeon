Hanuman Chalisa & Pregnanacy

Pregnancy is a time of great joy. It is a wonderful and precious occasion.

Most of the times it ends with delivery of a healthy baby. But sometimes it may end with giving birth to a defective baby (Birth Defects). Modern medicine is not able to find the cause but in our ancient science (Vedas & Upanishads) it was mentioned that “thought driven emotional stress” before and after conception is the root cause of the Birth defects.

Yoga is not just asanas (postures). Yoga is the way of living, our ancient science described to handle stress in day to day life. Bhakti Yoga, one of the four major categories of Yoga, is the simplest and easiest path to follow. “Hanuman Chalisa” is the simplest and easiest prayer to increase our devotion (Bhakti) which indirectly reduces our emotional stress.

It was scientifically proved that chanting of “Om” have beneficial effects on health of both the mother and baby. “Hanuman Chalisa & Pregnancy” is a programme designed to make the mothers experience the effect of reciting hanuman Chalisa and also to evaluate the effect on the growth and development of the baby.

To create awareness and enrolment in this programme, we are conducting Hanuman Chalisa contest to pregnant ladies and recently married couples (married within one year).
