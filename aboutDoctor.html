Dr G Venkata Subbarao

MS, MCh (Pediatric Surgery)

Consultant Neonatal and Pediatric Surgeon

Dr G Venkata Subbarao, Consultant Neonatal and Pediatric surgeon did his MBBS in Siddhartha medical college, Vijayawada.

He completed his Master's in General Surgery in Premiere Institute Osmania medical College, Hyderabad.

He worked as an Assistant professor in Mamatha medical College to satisfy his zeal in training young surgeons in addition to discharging his duties as a surgeon.

His compassion towards the suffering of children made him opt for Pediatric surgery as a Superspeciality. He did his Magistrate Chirurgie in Pediatric Surgery from the prestigious Niloufer hospital for women n children, one of the best exclusively Pediatric teritiary hospitals in India. He was trained in Minimal Access Surgery at Cemast Mumbai.

He is always enthusiastic in updating himself with the latest and recent advances by attending workshops and conferences. He works on the principle of 5D'S (Dedication, Devotion , Disciple and Discrimination and Determination) in his regular practice. His areas of interest are Minimal access surgery and neonatal surgery. His Vision is To work together with obstetricians and pediatricians for early detection and management of congenital anomalies to get good results for the upcoming youngsters. To educate and engage parents in decision making and management of their children's medical ailments.
