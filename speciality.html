My Speciality - Pediatric Surgery

  • Pediatric surgery is a subspecialty of surgery involving the surgery of foetuses(Unborn baby), neonates (New born baby), infants (upto one year), children (2 – 12 years), adolescents (13 – 19 years).
  • Children are not just small adults. They cannot always say what is bothering them. They cannot always answer medical questions, and are not always able to be patient and helpful during a medical examination. Their problems are also different for adult’s. We are specially trained as a Pediatric Surgeons in institutes exclusively that take care of New-born & children with overall duration of 11 ½ - 12 years in recognised medical colleges (MBBS 5 ½ years, MS general Surgery – 3 years, M.Ch Pediatric Surgery – 3 years)
